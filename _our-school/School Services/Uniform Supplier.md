<p><strong>Shanghai School Uniforms Pte Ltd</strong>&nbsp;has been supplying
school uniforms to Singapore schools for the past 45 years and is one of
the leading suppliers in the nation. We believe that quality school attire
play a role in the learnng environment as dressing smartly is part and
parcel of good image and discipline at school.&nbsp;
<br>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our company's key selling points are our <strong><u>Quality</u></strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong><u>Value-added Services</u></strong>
</p>
<hr>
<h4>Quality Products</h4>
<p>We produce good quality school uniforms and attire.&nbsp; The quality
of our product is evident by the good workmanship, fine finish and superior
tailoring techniques, using only colour-fast and high durable fabrics.&nbsp;</p>
<hr>
<h4>Customer Service Policy</h4>
<p>Our service policy is that customer comes first.&nbsp; In this, we have
a dynamic team of experienced customer service staff to service our customers'
feedback/queries on all uniform matters promptly.&nbsp;&nbsp; Our team
is well-trained on uniform fitting/sizing and uniform care.</p>
<hr>
<h4>Stock Availability</h4>
<p>&nbsp;Stock availability is targeted at 95% during the peak period.&nbsp;
During the off-peak period, stock level is maintained at 90% with replenishment
within 3 to 7 working days.</p>
<hr>
<h4>Wide Range of Sizes</h4>
<p>To ensure that our students of every build are outfitted smartly, we cater
to a wide range of sizes for our school attire.&nbsp; In this way, we pride
ourselves to be the most competitive in the market.&nbsp;&nbsp; This save
costs for our customers by reducing the need to spend extra money on alterations.</p>
<h4>For the convenience of our customers, we offer various retail channels as follows:</h4>
<h4>1.&nbsp; Bookshop</h4>
<p>Arrangements have been made to place our uniforms in the school bookshop
for the convenience of the parents and students.</p>
<h4>2.&nbsp; School Sales</h4>
<p>We conduct our year end uniform sales in the school during the peak period
from mid-November to mid-January and our schedule is pegged to the bookshop's
schedule (date and timing).&nbsp;&nbsp; For Primary 1 orientation, we deploy
maximum resources (a team of 10-15 staff) to carry out sales to the incoming
students.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Notwithstanding our superior quality and service, we are mindful of our
costs and maintain competitive prices for our products. By having our own
manufacturing facilities and direct link with reputable international fabric
mills, we are able to keep a close watch on our manufacturing costs while
maintaining high quality standards so that our customers get the <strong>best value for money.</strong>
</p>
